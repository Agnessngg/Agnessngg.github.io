OUTER BANKS

Outer Banks is a Netflix series where teen John B enlists his three best friends to hunt for a legendary treasure linked to his father's disappearance. Throughout the series, the teens discover that the other treasures they search for are all connected to the first treasure they found.

I like this series because it's a mix of mystery and adventure. The plot is always moving, intense, and filled with lots of twists. Throughout the show, all the characters undergo major development, and their experiences shape them into who they are.
